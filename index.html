What is SiriCoin?

SiriCoin is a digital currency that is based on the beaconchain. Our currency is designed to work seamlessly with other Web3 technologies, making it an ideal choice for users who want to take advantage of the latest advancements in blockchain technology.

With SiriCoin, you can easily make transactions, buy and sell NFTs, participate in DeFi protocols, and much more.

Feature's Of SiriCoin

Thor Transactions

SiriCoin enables lightning/thor transactions, which are lightning-fast transactions that are processed in seconds.
This means that you can send and receive SiriCoin quickly and easily, without having to wait for long confirmation times.

DeFi Protocols

SiriCoin is also compatible with DeFi protocols, which are decentralized finance applications that enable users to lend, borrow, and earn interest on their digital assets. With SiriCoin, you can easily participate in DeFi protocols and earn rewards for your contributions.

Smart Contracts

SiriCoin is also designed to work seamlessly with smart contracts, which are self-executing contracts that are stored on the blockchain.
With SiriCoin, you can easily create and execute smart contracts, enabling you to automate complex business processes.

NFT's

SiriCoin is also compatible with NFTs, or non-fungible tokens.
NFTs are unique digital assets that are stored on the blockchain.
With SiriCoin, you can easily buy and sell NFTs, or even create your own.

Metaverse

SiriCoin is also compatible with the metaverse, which is a virtual world that is built on blockchain technology.
With SiriCoin, you can easily participate in the metaverse and buy and sell virtual assets.

Mine SiriCoin

SiriCoin is a CPU-based cryptocurrency, which means that it can be mined using your computer's processing power. This provides an eco-friendly alternative to energy-intensive mining methods.
